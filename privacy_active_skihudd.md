## Overview
Active SkiHUD respects your privacy.  
The app is designed to provide real-time skiing statistics and video recording while keeping all your data on your device.  
We do not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

## Data Collection
Active SkiHUD does not collect or upload personal user information to any external server.

*   **No account creation** required
*   **No advertising** within the app
*   **No analytics** or tracking SDKs used
*   **No background data uploading**

## Permissions & Usage

### 1. Location (GPS)
The app requires access to your device's location to calculate:
*   Speed (km/h)
*   Altitude
*   Distance traveled
*   Slope gradient

**Privacy Note:** Location data is processed entirely **locally** on your device. It is not tracked, stored, or sent to the developer or third parties unless you explicitly export it.

### 2. Camera & Microphone
The app uses the camera and microphone to:
*   Display the HUD overlay (Augmented Reality view)
*   Record videos of your ski runs with overlay metrics

**Privacy Note:** Videos are stored **locally** in your device's gallery or app storage. The app does not upload these videos automatically.

### 3. Physical Activity / Sensors
The app uses device sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) to calculate:
*   G-Force
*   Device orientation

## Internet Usage
An internet connection is used **only** for:
*   Displaying map tiles in the analysis view.

Core tracking and recording functionality works completely **offline**.

## Data Storage & Sharing
All data (telemetry logs, GPX files, videos) is stored locally on your device.

*   You retain full ownership of your data.
*   You can choose to **export** your runs (as CSV, GPX, or Video) via the standard system share sheet. This sends the data only to the app or contact you select.

## Third-Party Services
Active SkiHUD avoids external tracking services.
*   **Maps:** Map visualization is provided by OpenStreetMap contributors. Loading map tiles requires a standard internet connection, subject to OpenStreetMap's data usage policy.

## Childrenâ€™s Privacy
Active SkiHUD is safe for all ages.
*   We do not collect personal data.
*   No social features or public sharing options exist within the app itself.

## Changes to This Policy
If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this location.

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer via GitHub:
https://github.com/leensen
