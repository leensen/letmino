## Overview

Mentori Study respects your privacy.  
The app is designed to minimize data usage and does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

Some features optionally use external AI services to process learning content provided by the user.

---

## Data Collection

Mentori Study does **not** collect or store personal user information such as names, email addresses, account details, or device identifiers.

- No account creation  
- No advertising  
- No analytics  
- No tracking  

---

## Camera & OCR Usage

The app allows users to take photos of learning materials (such as homework or textbooks).

- Images are processed **only** to extract text (OCR)  
- Images are **not stored** by the app  
- OCR results are processed temporarily to support learning features  

---

## AI Processing (Optional)

When the user chooses to:

- summarize extracted text  
- generate explanations  
- create practice questions  

the OCR text is sent securely to an external AI service selected by the user.

### AI Services Used

- Bring Your Own LLM via **Microsoft AI Foundry**

**Important notes:**

- Only the OCR text content is sent  
- No personal identifiers are added by the app  
- The developer does not store or log this data  

Processing is subject to the privacy and data handling policies of the selected AI provider, including Microsoft.

---

## Internet Usage

- An internet connection is **only required** when AI features are used  
- Core app functionality remains available without internet access  

---

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Homework planning  
- Learning progress  
- Rewards / stars  
- App preferences  

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use AI features.

---

## Third-Party Services

Mentori Study does **not** use:

- advertising networks  
- analytics tools  
- tracking SDKs  

The only third-party interaction is the **optional AI processing** initiated by the user.
